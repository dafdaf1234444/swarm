# L-1167: Self-improvement shows maturation not compounding — quantity decelerates while quality-per-unit improves
Session: S468 | Domain: expert-swarm | Level: L3 | Confidence: 0.75 | Cites: L-1121, L-1158, L-895, L-1131
Sh: 8

Falsification test of "self-improvement compounds over time" across 3 eras (S341-S400, S401-S450, S451-S468). Quantity metrics decelerate monotonically: DOMEX yield 1.03→0.82→0.59 L/lane, lessons/session 5.9→4.8→3.6, merges/session 3.8→3.4→2.7. But quality metrics improve: K→P ratio 15.5→9.3→8.4 (principle extraction more efficient), L3+ rate among tagged lessons 21.5%→81.0%.

**Pattern**: r/K ecological transition (L-1121). The swarm's knowledge DENSITY increases even as production VOLUME declines. Compounding is real but operates on quality, not quantity. This is consistent with NK crystallization (L-1158) — the graph matures rather than grows explosively.

**Prediction**: If maturation model is correct, yield should stabilize in Era 4 (S469-S518) around 0.4-0.6 L/lane (not further decline). Continued decline below 0.3 would indicate genuine decay, not maturation.

**Confounds**: Era 3 small sample (n=18 sessions), L3+ infrastructure introduced mid-Era 2, concurrent sessions deflate per-session metrics.
