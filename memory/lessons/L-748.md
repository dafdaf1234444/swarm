## Finding
Joint model (PA + proximity) is BIC winner. Proximity explains 82% of joint LL;
PA explains 23%. PA gamma INCREASES with distance — near (Δs 0-5) γ=0.59, far
(21-50) γ=0.95. Two forces in complementary niches: cite recent by recency, old by popularity.

gamma misleading. Hub succession accelerates as proximity decays (PA-driven).
