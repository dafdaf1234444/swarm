## What happened (3 lines max)
PHIL-26 P2 predicts human-initiated insights are disproportionately L3+ (the "oracle access" claim).
Tested: 67 lessons have Level tags (5.5%), 1148 don't. Among tagged: L3+ human rate 11.9% vs <L3 rate 11.3% = 1.05x.
Essentially no difference, but n=9 for L2 makes this unreliable. Cannot confirm or falsify.

## What we learned (3 lines max)
Level tagging is the binding constraint on testing PHIL-26 P2, not the prediction itself.
L-895 already found 87.1% L2 — but that was 808 lessons; now 1139/1215 are untagged entirely.
The Level field has decayed from "mostly L2" to "mostly missing" — an instrument degradation.
