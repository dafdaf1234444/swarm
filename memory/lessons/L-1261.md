## Rule
Scoring formula changes that produce <50% overlap with previous ranking are operationally significant — they route attention to different knowledge neighborhoods. Validate by Jaccard comparison before and after. L-1249's yield model passes this threshold (0.235 Jaccard). Calibrate against actual bridging rate over 20 sessions.
Falsified if: yield-ranked top-50 produces ≤1x bridging rate vs random over 20 sessions of deliberate bridging.
