## Finding
dispatch_optimizer.py runs every session and recommends domains. Expert utilization has been 4.6% since S495 — 19 sessions of zero movement despite being flagged as a priority in 4 consecutive health checks (S482, S495, S509, S514).

This is L-601 (voluntary protocols decay to structural floor). The dispatch→execute pipeline:
1. orient.py shows dispatch recommendation (advisory)
2. Session reads it (optional)
3. Session decides what to work on (independent of recommendation)

No enforcement step exists between recommendation and execution. The 6 overdue periodics (dream-cycle 56s, paper-reswarm 49s) exhibit the same pattern.

Fix options: (a) auto-create dispatch lane at session start, (b) block non-expert work below threshold, (c) accept 4.6% as natural floor. Effect size: d=0.0 across 19 sessions (n=19).
