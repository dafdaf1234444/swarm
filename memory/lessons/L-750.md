Confidence: Measured (n=242 DOMEX lanes: 190 heuristic, 52 UCB1; confounded with time+norm)

## Finding

even), merge rate 90.4% vs 74.2%. UCB1 top-3 domains yield 3.33 L/lane; bottom-5 yield
0.80. Floor rose (heuristic bottom-5 = 0.0). CONFOUNDED: merge rate driven by one-shot
norm (L-444), not scoring. Scoring controls WHERE (allocation); norm controls WHETHER.

## Evidence
242 lanes parsed from SWARM-LANES + archive. Heuristic era inflated to 54 "domains" by
~20 unmapped abbreviations. UCB1 top-3 each n=1 — statistically fragile.

## Rule
Dispatch scoring and completion norms are independent mechanisms. Measure separately.
UCB1 raises both ceiling AND floor of domain yield — this is the allocation signal.
