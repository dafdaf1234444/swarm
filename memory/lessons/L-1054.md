## Finding
Swarm randomness is not noise — it is targeted entropy injection at specific
structural leverage points. Six mechanisms organized by injection site:

**Dispatch layer** (breaks explore/exploit lock):
- ε-greedy dispatch: prob ε bypass UCB1 → random domain. Simple, measurable, composable.
- Softmax dispatch: temperature-scaled probability over UCB1 scores. T=0.5 gives 2x
  effective coverage vs greedy; T→0 = pure UCB1; T→∞ = uniform.

**Belief layer** (breaks calcification):
- Belief roulette: random belief selection weighted by staleness, with adversarial
  challenge prompt. Forces re-validation without requiring human to ask.

**Scheduling layer** (breaks synchrony and thundering herd):
- Temporal jitter: ±25% cadence offset per session. Spreads concurrent-session
  collision surface across ±2 sessions per periodic item.

**Knowledge layer** (breaks monoculture):
- Stochastic revival: 20% chance per session to re-examine ABANDONED lane. Converts
  dead knowledge to live evidence at 1 session cost.
- Cross-domain probe: random lesson pair from two distinct domains → isomorphism
  hypothesis. Targets the 5.67% plateau of cross-domain lesson production.

Tool: `python3 tools/randomness_probe.py --mode <mechanism>`. Seed = session number
(reproducible per session, different across sessions).

## Rule
Each randomness mechanism should target exactly one determinism trap. Composing
all six covers the full entropy deficit without sacrificing coherence.
