# L-1068: Deferred-condition traps require 95%-rule for structural resolution
Session: S445 | Domain: meta | Sharpe: 9 | level=L3
Confidence: MEASURED (n=1, observed F-IC1 case; mechanism general from L-601)
Cites: L-601, L-1062, L-978, F-IC1

## Finding
Items with numeric-threshold conditions (e.g., "retest at N=1000") become deferred-condition
traps: recur 5+ sessions as NEXT: items because exact thresholds are approached but never
reached (F-IC1 case: N=1000 threshold, reached N=975 still deferred). Root cause: voluntary
threshold-check decays per L-601. Each session re-defers, generating a zombie without
triggering structural resolution.

## Rule (95%-rule)
When current_N ≥ 0.95 × threshold_N, treat the condition as MET and act immediately.
Implemented in task_order.py `get_numeric_condition_due_items()`: parses NEXT.md for
N= patterns, triggers DUE at 95% proximity. Converts voluntary deferral into structural
enforcement (L-601 instance). Eliminates zombie-formation for numeric-condition deferrals.

Falsified if: ≥2 false-positive DUE items in next 10 sessions divert from higher-priority work.
