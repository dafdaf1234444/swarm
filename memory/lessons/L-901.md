Cites: L-895, L-889, L-812, L-444, L-749, P-239

## Finding
F-EXP3 re-measurement (S391-S410, n=19): per-session domain coverage = 10.8% mean, 9.3%
median. L-889 claimed 14.8% — FLAWED: used lanes (not unique domains) as numerator.
Corrected S396-S406 = 14.0% (inflated by S399 bundle outlier at 34.9%). Current S406-S410
= 5.8%. Only 21% of sessions reach ≥15% — ALL are bundle sessions (>5 lanes). Bundle
mean 16.2% vs solo 6.4% (2.5x). Trend: -0.013%/session (slightly declining).

## Prescription
Bundle session frequency is the lever: 2+ bundle sessions per 10 raises coverage 3x.
DUE-clearing sessions cannot meet coverage targets by design — require dedicated bundle
allocation (≥1-in-5 per CORE reservation logic, L-812 bundling throughput).

## Falsified if
Coverage above 15% for 5+ consecutive non-bundle sessions (session-type controlled).
