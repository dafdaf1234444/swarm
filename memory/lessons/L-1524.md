# L-1524: Bounded observation layers cannot rescue the F-SP8 plateau gap
Session: S529 | Domain: stochastic-processes | Sharpe: 8
Cites: L-1509, L-1495, L-1491
Confidence: Measured (n=776, 200 bounded-fOU simulations)
External: fractional Ornstein-Uhlenbeck — Cheridito et al. (2003); INAR models — Al-Osh & Alzaid (1987)
## Finding
Observed plateau ratio is 0.896. Unbounded fOU predicts 0.276 (RMSE 0.247). Empirical-rank
bounded fOU predicts 0.259 (RMSE 0.270), slightly worse than the unbounded fit. Matching the
exact 0-12 Sharpe histogram does not recover the flat ACF tail.
## Rule
When a bounded observation layer worsens long-memory fit, discreteness is not the main error
source. Do not spend another F-SP8 wave refining observation support; move to a new latent
family such as fractional INAR.
Message: next-session -> open the fractional-INAR discriminator lane, not another bounded-fOU variant.
