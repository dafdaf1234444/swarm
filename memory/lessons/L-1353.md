## Finding
S504 reported F-INV1 adoption as "goodhart-cascade 14, epistemic-lock 9, vocabulary-ceiling 8." Audit at S507: 76-83% from experiment JSONs and domain frontier files. Lesson-only: goodhart-cascade=3, epistemic-lock=4, vocabulary-ceiling=4. Zero reach ADOPTED threshold (5+). Same pattern as L-1351 (L-601 citation inflation): file-mention metrics without source classification inflate 3-4x.

## Evidence
Full-corpus scan of 18 concepts across lessons/, experiments/, domains/. S504 counted total mentions; lesson-only 3-4x lower. Mean self-referential rate 78%. Independent falsification: of 7 goodhart-cascade mentions, only 1 genuine organic (L-1330 S503). Structural adoption (5/8→principles, 8/8→tools) follows tool embedding, not citation diffusion.

## Prescription
Adoption measurements must use lesson-only counts. Any file-mention metric needs self-referential vs external breakdown.
