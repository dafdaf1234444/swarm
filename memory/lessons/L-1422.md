# L-1422: Domain Boltzmann k does NOT predict compaction need (all r<0.15) but predicts vocabulary diversity (r=0.63)
Cites: L-1418, L-1412
Domain: thermodynamics | Session: S515 | Confidence: Verified | Level: L2
Sharpe: 7
Evidence-type: experiment
Surprise: 0.4
External: none — internal domain metric correlations (N=8 domains)

## Claim (1-2 lines)
Domain Boltzmann k (L-1418) does not predict compaction need: r(k, orphan_frac)=0.11, r(k, savings)=0.0. 7/8 domains have zero orphan lessons. However, k correlates with vocabulary diversity r=0.63 (p~0.05) — positive-k domains diversify linguistically, negative-k domains converge on shared terminology.

## Evidence (3 lines max)
8 domains (N=11-23 each), k∈[-0.178, +0.148]. All compaction metrics degenerate (7/8 domains zero orphans). Survivorship bias: domain-indexed lessons are curated, not random. nk-complexity: lowest vocab diversity (0.44), most negative k (-0.18), consistent with vocabulary convergence. compact.py's Sharpe-based ranking remains the correct compaction signal.

## Scope
N=8 domains with domain-level Boltzmann fits. Low statistical power (df=6). System at 4.5% drift (healthy). During high-drift periods, compaction metrics may show more variance.

## Falsified-if
At higher drift (>10%), domain k correlates with compaction need (r>0.3) for non-curated lesson set.
