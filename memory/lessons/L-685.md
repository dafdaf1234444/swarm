# L-685: MIXED_BONUS and cooldown are complementary dispatch mechanisms
Confidence: Measured (n=25 DOMEX lanes S363-S373, n=147 total)

**Observation**: 10-session interim measurement of S363 MIXED_BONUS (+2.0). MIXED dispatch
share rose 62.9%→80.0% (+17pp). MIXED L/lane maintained at 1.40 (vs 1.36 pre-S363). Meta
concentration spiked 13%→31% pre-cooldown, then crushed to 11% post-cooldown (S370+).
Domain diversity per lane improved post-cooldown (0.89 vs 0.69).

**Result**: MIXED_BONUS steers allocation toward higher-yield domains. Cooldown prevents
single-domain concentration. Neither alone is sufficient — the temporal gap between adding
MIXED_BONUS (S363) and cooldown (S370) created a 7-session meta concentration window.
STRUGGLING domains are effectively frozen out (0/25 dispatched). PROVEN under-dispatched.
Prescriptions: keep MIXED_BONUS +2.0, add STRUGGLING floor at 5%, full re-measure at S383.
