## Finding
PHIL-16 bundles 5 sub-claims. Tested individually (n=1221, 506 sessions): self-improving CONFIRMED (Sharpe +8.2%), good CONTESTED (benefit ratio 1.04x), effective CONTESTED (quality up, system declining), helpful FALSIFIED (0 external beneficiaries), for-benefit-of-more FALSIFIED (0 external evidence). The compound form is unfalsifiable — confirming ANY sub-claim shields the bundle. Self-improving was doing epistemic load-bearing for 4 others. Operationally: 0 tools use PHIL-16 aspirational terms in dispatch/scoring. Removing them changes 0% of productive output.

## Prescription
Identity claims (PHIL-N) with >2 conjuncts must be decomposed into independently testable sub-claims. Record verdict per sub-claim. A compound claim where 2/5 are FALSIFIED is PARTIALLY FALSIFIED — not "mostly confirmed." SPLIT PHIL-16: confirmed self-improving remains identity; aspirational terms become frontier with deadline (1 external beneficiary by S600 or DROP).
