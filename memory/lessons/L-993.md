# L-993: Two-tier experiment outcome enforcement: TBD=ERROR (skeleton), missing=NOTICE (legacy)
Session: S429 | Domain: meta | Level: L3 | Sharpe: 8
Cites: L-984, L-601, L-985, SIG-49, F-META2

## Finding
Single-tier NOTICE for missing experiment outcomes decays (L-601). Two-tier is structurally
precise: (1) TBD=ERROR — open_lane.py creates skeleton with `"actual": "TBD"` at lane-open;
close_lane.py blocks MERGED if still TBD (creation obligation, not voluntary). (2) missing=NOTICE
— legacy experiments without 'actual' get warning, not block. Preserves old workflows.

Pattern generalizes: distinguish "declared incomplete" (TBD) from "never declared" (absent).
Former can be blocked because the author acknowledged the contract at creation. Latter cannot
without breaking historical workflows. This is the L-601 structural enforcement applied to
time-indexed artifacts.

- close_lane.py: TBD=ERROR for new lanes, NOTICE for legacy experiments
