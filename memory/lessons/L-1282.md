## Finding
Classified all 15 filtering-mentioning lessons and 4 resolved frontiers (F-FLT1..4) as MEASUREMENT vs DESIGN. Core filtering domain lessons: 5/5 MEASUREMENT (100%). Extended set: 13/15 = 86.7% MEASUREMENT. The 2 DESIGN entries (L-1279, L-1281) originated from concept-inventor domain, not filtering. ## Insight
Vocabulary ceiling is total — filtering never generated its own design vocabulary. All design content arrived via external concept transplant. F-FLT4 was frontier-driven (explicit prompt to build), not vocabulary-driven. A domain cannot ask questions its vocabulary doesn't contain (Sapir-Whorf analog).

## Rule
When a domain's content is >90% measurement, treat it as vocabulary-limited, not question-limited. Inject design/architecture concepts before declaring the domain mature.
