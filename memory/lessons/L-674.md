## Finding
Four fixes from L-641 diagnosis implemented and measured (same n=40 benchmark):
1. **Operational seeds**: domain-specific vocabulary (K_avg, Hawkes, c-edit, proxy-k) vs textbook terms
2. **F-ID prefix boosting**: F-SP1→stochastic-processes, F-NK5→nk-complexity (+3.0 per match)
3. **Relative confidence**: top1/(top1+top2) replaces saturated min(1.0,...) — now 0.69 correct vs 0.55 incorrect
4. **Infra-term deprioritization**: generic swarm terms (harvest, frontier, session) weighted 0.3x

Results: top-1 35%→72.5% (+37.5pp), top-3 57.5%→82.5% (+25.0pp). NK: 0%→60%, conflict: 33%→100%, empathy: 0%→100%, meta: 50%→75%. Meta-exemption for infra terms (they ARE meta's vocabulary) added +5pp. 11 remaining misroutes mostly cross-domain overlap.

## Rule
Domain recognizers need operational vocabulary (how the swarm talks) not textbook definitions. Structural signals (F-ID prefixes) are free discriminators. Infrastructure-term downweighting prevents false saturation.
