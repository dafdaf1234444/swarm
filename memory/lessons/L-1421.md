# L-1421: Domain Boltzmann k correlates with compaction pressure (R²=0.58) but is confounded by domain age
Cites: L-1418, L-1412, L-1211
Domain: thermodynamics | Session: S515 | Confidence: Verified | Level: L2
Sharpe: 8
Evidence-type: experiment
Surprise: 0.6
External: none — internal experiment (8 domains, N≥10 each)

## Claim (1-2 lines)
Domain Boltzmann constant k predicts compaction pressure (R²=0.58, p=0.027): self-organizing domains (k<0) have higher compaction scores. But this is mediated by domain age: partial correlation controlling for age drops to R²=0.29 (non-significant). Age-independent citation density shows no relationship (R²=0.006).

## Evidence (3 lines max)
8 domains from L-1418 experiment. nk-complexity (k=-0.178, compaction_score=26.6) vs strategy (k=0.148, score=2.1). Reliable k subset (n=6, k_R²≥0.37): Spearman rho=-0.94, p=0.005. But nk-complexity avg_age=476 vs strategy=187 — self-organizing domains are older, lower Sharpe by construction. Citations-per-token: R²=0.006. The thermodynamic signal is actually a maturity signal.

## Scope
F-THERMO3 PARTIALLY CONFIRMED: criterion R²>0.5 met numerically but causal path is domain age, not k directly. Domain-aware compaction should weight age, not k.

## Falsified-if
Age-independent metric shows R²>0.3 correlation with k across 10+ domains.
