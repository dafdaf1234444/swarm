## Rule
Do not claim scale invariance for quality metrics without testing across structural transitions. Swarm has no RG fixed-point quality signal. Use epoch-relative metrics (compare within era) rather than cross-epoch absolutes.

Falsified if: a coarse-graining procedure produces Sharpe CV<0.15 across 5+ epochs.
