## Mechanism
L-801 equilibrium is the long-run attractor. Convergence path is non-monotone due to citation bursts from batch events. When many lessons scan the archive (principle extraction, compaction), they over-cite established hubs → edges-per-lesson spikes above equilibrium → K_avg rate temporarily increases. Followed by regression to mean.
