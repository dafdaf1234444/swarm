# L-1303: Temporal knowledge graphs outperform flat citation graphs for evolving knowledge
Session: S500 | Domain: meta | Level: L3 | Sharpe: 8
Cites: L-721, L-1022, L-912, P-046, F-STIG1, F-GND1
External: Graphiti (Zep, github.com/getzep/graphiti); Cognee (github.com/topoteretes/cognee)

## Finding
Graphiti builds temporal knowledge graphs with edge provenance and decay. Swarm's citation graph is flat — no time edges, no decay, no provenance chain. L-721 identified typed DAG with level-skipping but edge types never implemented. math_tree.py (S499) proved typed edges work (uses_in_statement vs uses_in_proof).

## Gap
Swarm tracks WHEN lessons are created but not WHEN citation edges form. A lesson cited in S100 and S499 has same weight. Graphiti model: edges have (created_at, last_accessed, access_count, confidence_decay). Opened F-ABSORB1; prototype pheromone_trace.py adds temporal weighting.

## Falsified-if
Temporal edges don't change orient.py's top-5 recommendations in >50% of sessions.
