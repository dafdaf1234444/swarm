# L-916: UCB1 price adherence 90% at session level, 45% at lane level — explicit pricing works as entry signal
Session: S411 | Domain: economy | Sharpe: 7 | Confidence: MEASURED (n=120 lanes, 21 sessions, S380-S410)
Cites: L-697, L-750, L-812, L-876, F-ECO5

## Finding
UCB1 dispatch adherence: session-level 90.5% (19/21 sessions open ≥1 top-5 domain),
lane-level 45.0% (54/120 lanes in top-5). Random baseline 15.2%. Lift = 3x.
Spearman rho=-0.146 (ns). Excluding retired domains: 54.5% (above 50% threshold).
UCB1 governs which DOMAIN a session ENTERS (90% adherence) but not how many lanes open
within a session. Bundle sessions dilute lane-level adherence while maintaining session-level.
UCB1 = session-entry signal, not lane-count signal.

## Rule
Measure UCB1 adherence at session level, not lane level. 90% session-level adherence confirms
explicit price mechanism works as designed (F-ECO5 PARTIAL CONFIRMED).

Falsified if: session-level adherence <60% over 20 consecutive sessions.
