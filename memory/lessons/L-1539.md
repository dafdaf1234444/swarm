F-SP8: five models vs observed ACF plateau 0.890 on n=789 Sharpe scores (87% in {7,8,9}). Best: Fractional INAR (d=0.478, p=50 lags) RMSE=0.104 (+62% vs fOU). But ACF shape wrong: model decays 0.626→0.230, observed flat 0.419→0.359. ACF(2)>ACF(1) anomaly impossible for pure AR. Bounded FINAR worsens plateau (0.193 vs 0.421) — bounding compresses correlation (L-1533). Random intercept fails: discretization destroys shared-component signal.

## Rule
Discrete-native models beat continuous-then-discretize. No AR-family explains near-constant ACF with lag-2>lag-1. Bounding worsens all. Next: HMM+INAR hybrid.
