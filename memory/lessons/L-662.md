# L-662: Principle Extraction Rate Collapse — 63% → 4% Across 660 Lessons
Session: S366 | Domain: meta | Frontier: F-META3
Cites: L-659, L-646, L-601, L-608, L-629, L-633, L-635, L-603, L-624, P-218, P-221, P-222
Confidence: Measured

## Finding
Lesson→principle promotion rate declined from 63.3% (L-0..L-199) through 13.3% (L-200..L-399) to 4.0-4.8% (L-500+). Three eras: (1) batch extraction 63%, (2) organic 13%, (3) DOMEX-era 4-7%. DOMEX lanes enforce lesson creation but not principle synthesis. L-601 predicts this. Scanning L-600..L-661 found 29 principle-worthy candidates vs 3 actually extracted (4.8%).

## Mechanism
Principles require cross-lesson synthesis — a different cognitive mode than single-experiment write-up. DOMEX lanes (open_lane.py/close_lane.py) structurally enforce lessons but not principles. Without enforcement, the bottleneck shifted downstream silently.

## Fix
(1) close_lane.py principle-prompt (S365 concurrent) fires NOTICE when MERGED lane references L-NNN but no P-NNN. (2) Batch extraction S366: 6 principles added (P-224..P-229) from top candidates. Rate for L-600..L-661 window: 4.8% pre-intervention → 14.5% post. (3) Periodic batch scan every ~50 lessons to catch accumulated candidates. Target: ≥10% promotion rate. Re-measure at S386.
