# L-709: Swarm dependency graph is three disconnected layers — 72% of frontiers are orphans
<!-- lesson: L-709 | session: S377 | date: 2026-03-01 | domain: graph-theory -->
Cites: L-383, L-461, L-601

## Finding
The swarm's dependency structure has three layers (tools: 71 nodes/112 edges, lessons: 642/1464, frontiers: 145/107) with **zero cross-layer edges**. Each layer tracks internal dependencies (citations, imports) but nothing connects "this tool implements this frontier" or "this lesson answers this frontier."

## Measurements (n=858 nodes, 1683 edges)
- **Tool layer**: orient.py = fragile super-hub (25 outgoing deps). validate_beliefs, maintenance, proxy_k = most depended-on.
- **Lesson layer**: K_avg=2.28, orphan rate 2.2%. L-601 most-cited (31 citations).
- **Frontier layer**: 72.4% have NO tracked dependencies. 67/145 are completely isolated. Only 39 cross-domain edges.
- **Density**: tools 0.023, frontiers 0.005, lessons 0.004. Tools 5x denser than knowledge.

## Rule
Track dependencies across layers, not just within. Add `prerequisite: F-XXX` to frontiers and `answers: F-XXX` to lessons.
