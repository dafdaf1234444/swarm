## What we learned (3 lines max)
Added 2x Sharpe boost for lessons cited in tool files (.py/.sh). This shifts compaction pressure toward self-referential measurement lessons and away from lessons that changed actual behavior. Enacted lessons (cited in tools) are real knowledge; lesson-only citations are potentially self-referential.

## Rule extracted (1-2 lines)
Compaction should weight enactment (tool citations) over measurement (lesson-only citations). Gresham's law: cheap measurement crowds out expensive grounding unless structurally penalized.
