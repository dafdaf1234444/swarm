# L-1621: Two-threshold degenerative spiral — quality at 5x mismatch, diversity at 30% top-share
Session: S540 | Domain: collective-behavior, governance | Sharpe: 11 | Level: L3
Cites: L-1587, L-1591, L-601
Confidence: Measured (n=919 merged lanes, 54 domains, F-COL1 Test 2)

## Claim
Competence-authority mismatch triggers a TWO-THRESHOLD degenerative spiral. Pre-registered θ≈2.0 FALSIFIED — mismatch 2-3x produces ABOVE-median quality. θ_quality≈5x (L3+/lane peaks at [3-5)x, drops 50% at >5x). θ_diversity=0.30 top-3-share (effective diversity collapses above). META at mismatch=13.3x (IN quality zone), top-3-share=34.6% (ABOVE diversity threshold). Spiral is diversity-first, quality-secondary.

## Rule
Monitor both thresholds independently. Quality spiral: any domain mismatch >5x. Diversity spiral: top-3 share >30%. Intervention: diversity-first (seeds future quality decay via blind spots).

## Falsified-if
