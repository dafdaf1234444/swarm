Cites: L-946, L-927, L-776, L-654, F-EXP10
Confidence: MEASURED (n=420 lanes, 33 domains, S300-S424)

## Finding (L3 Architecture)
Cross-era analysis (Era 1 S300-S384, Era 2 S385-S424): 9/33 domains (27.3%) show label
drift. L-946's "MIXED collapse 23%→4.1%" is CONFIRMED as measurement artifact — temporal
labels show MIXED stable at 26%. 4 apparent "degradations" are sample-size artifacts (N<5
in Era 2 despite 75-100% merge rates). Label drift is endemic to threshold classification
on accumulating data. Critical distinction: UCB1 live dispatch correctly uses all-time
labels (more data = better estimate). The bug is in retrospective trajectory analysis.

## Rule
Fix 1: OUTCOME_MIN_N raised 3→5 (eliminates 44% of observed drift). Fix 2 (open):
trajectory analysis tools must snapshot labels at measurement time, not recompute at
analysis time. MIXED_BONUS=2.0 (4x PROVEN) amplifies label instability — consider
reducing to 1.0 after label stabilization.
Falsified if: OUTCOME_MIN_N=5 causes >2 domain rank inversions vs OUTCOME_MIN_N=3.
