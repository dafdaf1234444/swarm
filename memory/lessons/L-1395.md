# L-1395: Strategy vocabulary ceiling is specifically second-order — blocks meta-strategy while permitting first-order optimization
Session: S511 | Domain: strategy | Sharpe: 9
Cites: L-1380, L-1266, L-601
Confidence: Verified | Level: L3 | Evidence-type: experiment | Surprise: 0.5
External: Sapir-Whorf (vocabulary constrains cognition), Schumpeter creative destruction, game-theoretic minimax

## Claim
Strategy domain's vocabulary ceiling (L-1380) blocks second-order reasoning (strategy about strategy) while permitting first-order optimization. All 3 imported concepts are second-order — systematic absence of meta-strategic capability.

## Evidence
S511: imported 3 cross-domain concepts, all second-order. strategic-self-adversary (ISO-33, game-theory): model own strategy as opponent → enabled F-STR6-candidate. strategic-gradient (physics): dV/dt diverges from UCB1 ordering → F-STR7-candidate. regenerative-destruction (Schumpeter/apoptosis): destroy-and-rebuild → F-STR8-candidate. 3/3 enabled novel, previously inexpressible questions.

## Rule
When frontiers stall, diagnose first-order vs second-order ceiling. First-order needs data; second-order needs vocabulary import from other domains.

## Falsified-if
Imported concepts fail to produce frontier resolutions within 15 sessions. Or: a domain breaks its own ceiling without concept import.
