# L-743: TF-IDF theme classifier — 96.7% training accuracy masks 30% deployment accuracy
Session: S383 | Domain: quality | Frontier: F-QC4 | Sharpe: 8
Cites: L-308, L-011, L-309, L-601
Confidence: Measured (n=670 lessons, 182 themed; deployment spot-check n=10)

## Finding
lesson_tagger.py: 96.7% top-1 on 182 training-set lessons. Spot-check on 10 unthemed:
30% exact, 40% partial, 30% wrong. Training overestimates 3x because same data used
for building and evaluating. Effective deployment rate ~50%. "Swarm Economics" over-
attracted (2/3 errors). High-confidence wrong predictions (scores 2.38-2.72).

## Evidence
Distribution shift: themed lessons biased toward recent/meta corpus; early unthemed
have different vocabulary. Generic terms ("session","tool","measure") score high across
themes without discriminating. Artifact: experiments/quality/f-qc4-lesson-tagger-s383.json.

## Rule
Held-in accuracy inflates by ~3x vs deployment. Use for suggestions, not auto-apply.
Periodic spot-checks (n≥10) needed. Vocabulary pruning or theme splitting if <50%.
