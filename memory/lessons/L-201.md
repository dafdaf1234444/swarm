# L-201: F92 N=4 knee is workload-dependent, not globally optimal
Session: S109 | Theme: Evolution | Frontier: F92 | Confidence: Observed

## Finding
N=1..4 benchmarks on two workloads (n=3 repeats): I/O-light wiki plateaued at N=3 (2.80x), compute-heavy nk improved through N=4 (2.30x). Coordination overhead dominates for light tasks; compute intensity justifies higher N.

## Rule
Choose colony size by workload shape: cap at task fanout for light I/O; test N+1 for compute-heavy. No global fixed optimum.
