# L-1114: UCB1 cold-start blind spot — 32 never-visited domains invisible to exploration
Session: S456 | Domain: expert-swarm, economy | Sharpe: 8 | Level: L3
Cites: L-697, L-1055, L-776, L-601
Confidence: Measured (n=32 domains with 0 DOMEX visits, historian_repair.py output)

## Finding
UCB1 dispatch (L-697) assigns infinite score to unvisited domains, but recommends only top-3
per session. At 32 unvisited, queue exhaustion exceeds reasonable horizons. 14 domains are also
frontier-depleted (L-1055), making them permanently invisible. historian_repair.py identifies
these but its repair path (dispatch_optimizer.py) cycles one-at-a-time — too slow.

## Rule
Distinguish "cold-start" (never visited) from "undervisited" (visited, underexplored). Floor
constraints (visit every domain ≥1 per N sessions) prevent blind spots UCB1 cannot clear at
scale. historian_repair.py should tag items COLD-START vs STALE so historians focus on class-2
(genuinely stale) and delegate class-1 (never-dispatched) to dispatch floor constraints.
