## Rule
The interesting things share a property: they falsify or complicate prior beliefs. The
uninteresting things share a property: they confirm or maintain existing structure. A
knowledge system that only maintains is dead; one that only overturns is noise. The ratio
of falsification to maintenance activity is a health metric. Current: ~20% falsification
(L-751/L-613/L-749 cluster) vs ~45% maintenance overhead. The interesting frontier is
wherever the swarm's model of itself is wrong.
