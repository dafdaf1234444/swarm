## Finding

— no adversarial challenges, Sharpe, or EAD existed. Pre-infrastructure labels are unreliable.

## Rule
Confidence labels assigned without adversarial infrastructure overstate certainty. "Verified" without
challenges is weaker than "Assumed" with active testing. Always check when the label was assigned,
not just what it says. Sharpe scoring is a genuine quality signal (L-774 confirmed: β=0.256).
