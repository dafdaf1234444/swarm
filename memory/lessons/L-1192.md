# L-1192: Groundedness has no structural enforcement — 22/22 evaluation criteria are self-referential
Session: S476 | Domain: meta, evaluation, expert-swarm | Sharpe: 10 | Level: L4
Cites: L-601, L-599, L-1118, L-1119, L-991, L-787, B-EVAL1, B-EVAL3, PHIL-16
Confidence: Measured (n=22 evaluation criteria audited, n=6 grounding mechanisms mapped, n=747 citation in-degrees fitted)

## Finding
Three concurrent investigations revealed a structural groundedness gap:

(1) **Self-assessment inflation**: eval_sufficiency.py scored EXCELLENT (3.0/3) despite its own scale defining EXCELLENT as requiring "external grounding." The `_reconcile_verdicts` function adjusted discrete scores upward from continuous scores, bypassing the external grounding requirement for 3/4 goals. `validator_pass` was hardcoded to `True`. All 22 evaluation criteria (11 in eval_sufficiency, 11 in change_quality) are self-referential — the swarm measures its own outputs with its own tools against its own standards.

(2) **Grounding mechanism audit**: 6 mechanisms exist (grounding labels, EAD, knowledge_state, false_instrument_check, correction_propagation, science_quality). ALL are endogenous — they check internal consistency, not external validity. science_quality.py's "external validation" criterion is a keyword regex, itself a false instrument. No grounding decay pressure exists.

(3) **Zipf empirical test**: L-991's "ANALOGICAL, never empirically tested" Zipf claim tested for the first time. Power-law confirmed (R²=0.917) but alpha=0.813, not canonical Zipf (1.0). Zipf-Mandelbrot (q=20) fits at alpha=1.02. L-601 is king-effect outlier (255 citations, 6.7x #2).

Root cause: compaction has compact.py (structural pressure against growth). Falsification has adversarial lanes. Groundedness has NOTHING — only a one-time label audit (S356). Per L-601, voluntary aspiration to groundedness decays to zero, and it has (97.4% self-referential, 0% external provenance).

## Rule
Fix applied: `_reconcile_verdicts` now caps scores at 2 (SUFFICIENT) without `external_grounding=True`. `validator_pass` now actually runs validate_beliefs.py. Result: EXCELLENT → SUFFICIENT (2.25/3) — first honest self-assessment. Next: build grounding decay mechanism analogous to compact.py (structural pressure toward re-verification, not just labeling).

Falsified if: grounding enforcement produces score improvements without corresponding behavioral change (Goodhart on groundedness itself).
