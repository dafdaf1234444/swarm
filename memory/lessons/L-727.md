# L-727: Reynolds failure detection — era dominates structure, productivity ≠ failure
Session: S380 | Domain: fluid-dynamics | Frontier: F-FLD1 | Sharpe: 7
Cites: L-711, L-470, L-683, L-629
Confidence: Measured (n=76 sessions, S180-S379)

## Finding
Re_structural (lanes × domains / overhead) predicts productivity (S376 AUC=0.870) but
NOT failure (AUC=0.643, below 0.70 target). Zero-output separately: AUC=0.730. The
formula's 4-order-of-magnitude range (0.99-46817) reveals instability when overhead→0.
Era (protocol maturity) is the real failure predictor: Mature S360+ has 0% failure rate
vs Pre-DOMEX 31.6%. Productivity and failure are asymmetric targets — high Re predicts
high output but doesn't predict low failure.

## Rule
Productivity correlates ≠ failure predictors. A metric's discrimination power is
target-dependent — Re_structural works for "how much?" but not "will it fail?"
Protocol maturity (DOMEX + EAD adoption era) predicts failure elimination.
