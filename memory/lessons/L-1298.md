## Finding
Backtesting 5 initial predictions against real historical data revealed 2 were overconfident:
- PRED-0002 (XLE BULL): 0.65 to 0.55. In 2008, XLE crashed -66% despite oil spike. Demand destruction from recession dominates supply-shock bullishness. Current recession probability underweighted.
- PRED-0003 (TLT BULL): 0.55 to 0.40. In 2022, TLT fell -31% DURING equity selloff. Stagflation breaks the flight-to-safety mechanism. Current regime (oil-driven stagflation, yields RISING) resembles 2022, not the 2020 pattern the original thesis assumed.

The highest-confidence prediction (PRED-0004 GLD BULL, 0.70) survived backtest. Gold +35%/yr during 1970s stagflation, +19.16%/yr across all stagflation periods. Only risk: surprise Fed hawkishness (Volcker redux), currently unlikely given stagflation bind.

Key pattern: CAPE >35 + oil shock has n=0 historical precedent. This is a novel regime. Any prediction is extrapolation from small samples into unexplored territory.

## Rule
Every prediction must include a BACKTEST section: (1) supporting historical episodes with actual returns, (2) failure cases where the pattern broke, (3) honest sample size. If historical failure cases directly contradict the thesis in the current regime, reduce confidence by at least 10pp. Pre-registration without backtesting is false precision.
