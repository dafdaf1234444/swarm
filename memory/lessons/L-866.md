5 valley escapes (F-PSY3, F-FRA2, F-FRA3, F-SOC2, F-SOC3). Both F-STR3 resolution criteria

## Architecture
The "5-layer" escalation is really 2-level: domain selection (L1-L4: boost, floor, guarantee,
reservation) and frontier selection (L5: orient.py naming). L1-L4 achieved 80% domain coverage
but 0% frontier precision (L-845). L5 alone broke the domain→frontier gap. Within each level
the layers are incremental insurance, not redundant.

## Lesson
Frontier-level precision requires frontier-level naming. Domain-level dispatch solves coverage;
it cannot solve targeting. Two-level framing is more accurate than 5-layer sequential.
