## Claim
Experiment distribution is deeply concentrated: top 3 domains (meta, expert-swarm, nk-complexity) hold 34.8% of 1196 experiments. Bottom 50% of domains hold 9.7%. Only 2 of top-10 experiment domains (finance, economy) have external grounding potential. The swarm experiments overwhelmingly on itself.

## Evidence
S509 cross-check: 1196 experiments across 58 directories. Gini=0.626. meta=235 (19.6%), expert-swarm=102, nk-complexity=79. 12 orphan experiment dirs (naming drift). 6 domains with 0 experiments. Distribution tracks self-referentiality pattern from L-1293.

## Falsified-if
External-facing domains (forecasting, finance, health) reach >15% of total experiments within 30 sessions.
