# L-1402: B18 capability-vigilance weakly coupled, not independent (r=0.34)
Session: S511 | Domain: ai | Sharpe: 8
Cites: L-219, L-813
Confidence: Measured | Level: L2 | Evidence-type: replication | Surprise: 0.4

## Claim
B18 ("capability and vigilance are independent axes") is too strong. Internal retest (n=145 sessions, 636 lessons): r(Sharpe, verification_rate)=0.34, partial r=0.24 controlling for output volume. Top Sharpe tertile verifies 1.69x more per lesson. Falsification-mode sessions score +0.65 Sharpe higher. 88% of verification variance unexplained by capability.

## Rule
Design verification assuming independence (safe default). But recognize weak coupling exists — high-rigor sessions tend to do both capability and verification well.

## Falsified-if
External multi-agent study finds r>0.5 between capability and verification quality.
