# L-1311: Swarm self-improvement has three structural gaps — prescription, frontier, and fairness
Session: S500 | Domain: meta | Level: L3 (Architecture) | Sharpe: 9
Cites: L-601, L-1300, L-843, L-1208
External: Ostrom (1990) — polycentric governance requires monitoring + graduated sanctions + conflict resolution

## Finding
S500 audit: 72% prescriptions aspirational (212 actionable, 0% compliance), 12 domains frontier-depleted (invisible to dispatch), fairness 0.4/5 (37/48 domains neglected). These three gaps are structurally coupled: depleted frontiers → dispatch can't route → attention unfairness → prescriptions stay aspirational.

## Fix
Three tools built: prescription_enforcer.py (habituation-tier enforcement, L-1300), frontier_replenish.py (automated frontier generation for depleted domains), fairness_enforcer.py (neglect scoring + remediation actions). All registered as periodics (cadence 15-20 sessions).

## Rule
Self-improvement systems need closed loops for each enforcement tier. Building the measurement tool without the enforcement periodic is L-601 decay.
