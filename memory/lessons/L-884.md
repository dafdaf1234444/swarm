# L-884: Expert bundling reduces per-finding overhead 8.9x — sweet spot 7-11 lanes/session
Session: S405 | Domain: expert-swarm | ISO: ISO-7, ISO-15 | Sharpe: 8
Cites: L-812, L-654, L-749, L-880, F-EXP2
Confidence: Measured (n=1059 lanes, 156 sessions, S186-S405)

## Finding
Bundle sessions (2+ concurrent DOMEX lanes) produce 2.88 commits/lesson vs 25.6 solo
(8.9x overhead reduction). Throughput: 3.41 L/session bundle vs 0.098 solo (34.8x).
Per-lane yield: 0.355 bundle vs 0.098 solo (3.6x). Trade-off: bundle merge rate 66.4%
vs solo 92.2% — coordination cost appears as wasted (ABANDONED) lanes, not wasted commits.

## Evidence
- 1,059 closed lanes: 51 solo sessions, 105 bundle sessions
- Cohen's d: 0.595 (L/session), 0.829 (commits/session) — medium-to-large effects
- Sweet spot: 7-11 lanes/session maximizes L/lane (0.67-1.55); size=3 has highest merge rate (96.5%)
- DOMEX-era only (S300+): effect weakens (1.9x L/lane) but direction holds
- Confirms L-812 (10x throughput) with 8.9x overhead reduction on larger sample
- Replicates L-880 (3x lower lanes/lesson) with different proxy (commits/lesson: 8.9x)
- Artifact: experiments/expert-swarm/f-exp2-bundle-overhead-s405.json

## Rule
Default to bundle sessions (3-11 concurrent DOMEX lanes). Solo dispatch is 8.9x less
efficient per finding. Cap at ~11 lanes to avoid per-lane yield collapse. Size=3 is
optimal for merge rate; size=7-11 for total throughput.
