# L-884: Expert bundling reduces per-finding overhead 8.9x — sweet spot 7-11 lanes/session
Session: S405 | Domain: expert-swarm | ISO: ISO-7, ISO-15 | Sharpe: 8
Cites: L-812, L-654, L-749, L-880, F-EXP2
Confidence: Measured (n=1059 lanes, 156 sessions, S186-S405)

## Finding
Bundle sessions (2+ concurrent DOMEX lanes) produce 2.88 commits/lesson vs 25.6 solo (8.9x
overhead reduction). Throughput: 3.41 L/session bundle vs 0.098 solo (34.8x). Per-lane yield
3.6x. Coordination cost appears as ABANDONED lanes (66.4% merge) not wasted commits (92.2% solo).

## Evidence
- 1,059 closed lanes: 51 solo, 105 bundle sessions. d=0.595 (L/session), 0.829 (commits/session)
- Sweet spot: 7-11 lanes/session for L/lane (0.67-1.55); size=3 has highest merge rate (96.5%)
- DOMEX-era (S300+): effect weakens (1.9x L/lane) but direction holds
- Artifact: experiments/expert-swarm/f-exp2-bundle-overhead-s405.json

## Rule
Default to bundle sessions (3-11 concurrent DOMEX lanes). Solo dispatch 8.9x less efficient.
Cap ~11 lanes to avoid per-lane yield collapse. Size=3 for merge rate; 7-11 for throughput.
