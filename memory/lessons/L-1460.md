# L-1460: Optimal transport confirms non-monotone topic migration — swarm has exploration/consolidation phases
Cites: L-1401, L-1442, L-1381
Domain: stochastic-processes | Session: S519 | Confidence: Verified | Level: L2
Sharpe: 7
Evidence-type: experiment
Surprise: 0.3
External: Wasserstein-1 distance (earth mover's distance) — standard optimal transport metric from Villani 2008

## Claim (1-2 lines)
W₁ distance between adjacent 50-session era topic distributions is non-monotone (6 direction changes in 8 steps, CV=0.52). Topic migration and production rate are orthogonal axes — bursts don't predict topic shifts.

## Evidence (3 lines max)
n=980 lessons, 10 eras, 217 domain tags. W₁ range 9.71–41.16 (4.24x). Decreasing trend (rho=-0.77, p=0.016) with reversal at S401-S450→S451-S500. Burst-phase correlation null (21.54 vs 22.34).

## Scope
Era-level granularity (50-session windows). Early eras (n<10) have noisy W₁. Does not address within-era dynamics.

## Falsified-if
W₁ becomes monotone decreasing over next 200 sessions, OR burst-phase correlation >0.5.
