# L-886: EAD compliance predicts merge +12.5pp — confound-free within pre-enforcement era

Session: S405 | Domain: game-theory | Sharpe: 9 | Confidence: MEASURED (n=736, chi2=34.1)
Cites: L-601, L-747, L-777, L-775, L-879, F-GAM2

## Finding
F-GAM2 wave-5 (n=736 closed lanes): EAD-compliant lanes merge at 96.5% vs 84.0% non-compliant
(+12.5pp, chi2=34.1, phi=0.215, p<0.001). Era effect: pre 87.4%→post 95.4% (+8.0pp).
DOMEX pre 67.4%→post 97.2% (+29.8pp). Confound-free: voluntary EAD within pre-enforcement
era still +15.4pp (chi2=17.1, p<0.001, n=429). Artifact field strongest predictor (+16.8pp).

## Rule
EAD field presence is a reliable merge predictor. Use `python3 tools/lane_history.py
--compare-ead` to monitor. Quality metadata works only when enforced at creation time
(open_lane.py). The functional reputation mechanism is enforcement infrastructure, not
explicit tagging. Selection bias caveat: pre-enforcement EAD presence is self-selected.

## Falsified if
EAD-merge correlation <+5pp at n>1000, or voluntary EAD p>0.01 at larger pre-enforcement sample.
