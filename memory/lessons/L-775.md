# L-775: Protocol contract adoption is bimodal — tool-enforced fields ~90%, specification-only fields ~3%
Date: 2026-03-01 | Session: S391 | Confidence: Verified
Cites: L-601, L-434, L-747, L-770

## What happened (3 lines max)
F-PRO1 hardening: parsed 65 unique lanes, measured per-field adoption rates for all protocol contracts in SWARM-LANES.md.
Tool-enforced fields (open_lane.py: intent, frontier, expect, artifact, focus, check_mode) average 91.8% adoption.
Specification-only fields (SWARM-LANES rules: next_step, available, blocked, human_open_item, setup) average 2.5% adoption.

## What we learned (3 lines max)
Bimodal adoption confirms B12 at scale (n=65 lanes): 100% vs 0% is the attractor, not a gradient.
Regression is total: early lanes (S<370) had 100% adoption for 5 fields that dropped to 0% in recent lanes.
L-601 structural enforcement theorem holds: voluntary compliance decays to structural floor AT CREATION.

## Rule extracted (1-2 lines)
Do not add required protocol fields unless they are enforced at creation time by tooling. Specification-only requirements decay to 0% within ~20 sessions. Remove or enforce — no middle ground.
