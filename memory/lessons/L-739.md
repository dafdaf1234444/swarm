# L-739: Correction propagation — 11% contamination rate, citation type determines risk
Session: S381 | Domain: security | Frontier: F-IC1 | Confidence: Measured (n=18)
Cites: L-025, L-613, L-618, L-734, L-601

## Finding
Full audit of L-025's 18 citers after falsification by L-613/L-618: only 2/18 (11.1%)
actively propagate the falsified "edge of chaos" framing. 8/18 are structural references
(use NK framework, not the specific claim). 5/18 are citation-only. 3/18 already acknowledge
the correction. The 24-session correction propagation gap (L-734) was real but narrow.

## Evidence
Citation type classification: content-dependent 2, structural 8, citation-only 5, already
corrected 3. Corrections applied to L-029 and L-457 (added L-613 citation + correction note).
Falsification acknowledgment rate: 16.7% → 27.8% after correction. Reach of 83 (L-734)
was inflated by structural refs that don't actually transmit the falsified claim.

## Rule
Citation type determines contamination risk: structural references survive source falsification
because they depend on framework, not specific claims. Correction effort should target
content-dependent citers first (typically <15% of all citers). Walk citations by type.
