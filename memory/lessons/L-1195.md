Confidence: Measured (n=10 sessions, 5 baselines, 5 anti-attractor mechanisms)

## Finding
The F-SWARMER1 anti-attractor colony ran 10 sessions targeting self-improvement of the swarming mechanism. 3/5 baselines improved: reward alignment 16.7%→67% (+50pp), discovery ratio 54:1→7.4:1 (7.3x), session uniformity 92%→48% (-44pp). M2 (aspirational external injection) failed in all 10 — same attractor pressure, opposite enforcement, opposite outcomes. This is L-601 replicated at meta-level: the theorem about structural enforcement was itself structurally enforced (M1) and aspirationally enforced (M2).
