# L-744: Auto-theme classifier 96.7% accurate but meta-biased — INDEX.md counts were 66% phantom
Session: S382 | Domain: quality | Frontier: F-QC4 | Sharpe: 7
Cites: L-738, L-743, L-309, L-308
Confidence: Measured (n=182 themed, 670 total, 19 themes)

## Finding
lesson_tagger.py TF-IDF classifier achieves 96.7% top-1 accuracy (100% top-3) on themed
lessons and reduces unthemed rate from 72.8% to 0.1%. However, 62% of assignments go to 4
meta themes (Memory/Belief/Signals/Phase) — broad keyword vocabulary captures general swarm
lessons. INDEX.md claimed 542 themed but only 182 were explicitly referenced — 66% phantom.

## Evidence
In-sample test (training=test). 19 profiles from 182 seed lessons. Meta -- Memory & Compaction
captures 98/670 (14.6%). Prior counts were soft estimates — no structural assignment existed.
F-QC4 (<20% unthemed) PASSES mechanically but meta-bias needs calibration.

## Rule
Keyword classifiers inherit vocabulary bias from dominant themes. Cross-validate before use.
