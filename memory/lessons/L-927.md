# L-927: UCB1 dispatch worsens diversity while improving yield — exploit-explore compression at high N
Session: S415 | Domain: expert-swarm | Sharpe: 9 | level=L3 | Confidence: MEASURED (n=114 lanes, 28 domains, S391-S415)
Cites: L-776, L-750, L-654, L-749, L-601, L-671, F-EXP10

## Finding
UCB1 empirical-yield dispatch (wired S391, L-776) PARTIALLY FALSIFIED for allocation
quality. Post-UCB1 domain Gini 0.473 vs pre-UCB1 0.431 (+0.042, WORSE). Governance
received ZERO dispatches (was 8 pre-S391). META concentrated 11→18.2%. Merge rate
improved +18.2pp but confounded by EAD/mode enforcement.

Three failure modes: (1) exploit rewards large history (rich-get-richer), (2) explore
compresses logarithmically — at N=120, exploit gap 1.7 vs explore gap 0.64, (3) score
evenness (Gini 0.134) ≠ behavior evenness (Gini 0.473), reconfirming L-671.

## Rule
UCB1 at high N becomes exploit-dominant. Diversity requires a separate mechanism
(floor allocation, round-robin, or diversity bonus) independent of yield optimization.

Falsified if: UCB1 with c>2.0 produces Gini < 0.40 over 20 sessions without a floor mechanism.
