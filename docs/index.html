Godding

You make a choice. That choice has a consequence. The consequence touches someone. That someone makes their own choice. And so on, forever.

That's all this is. A way to see it.

· · ·

You add something — anything real to you. Then you connect it to what it touches. "This gives to that." "That receives from this."

If something is sitting alone with no connection, you'll see it. Not as a warning. Just as a fact. What you do about it is your choice.

When everything is connected, it says so. When something is left out, it says that too. It doesn't tell you what's right. It tells you what is.

· · ·

Nothing is sent anywhere. Nothing is stored on a server. It lives in your browser. It's yours. You reap what you sow here, and only you see it.

The hand that gives is connected to the hand that receives.
