Godding

The hand that gives is connected to the hand that receives.

What is this?

A place to put things down and connect them to each other. Ideas, people, problems, anything. You write them here, you draw the lines between them, and it tells you if anything got left out.

How does it work?

1. You add things. Type a name, press Add.
2. You connect them. Pick two, press Connect.
3. You see who gives to who, and who receives from who.
4. If something is left alone, it shows you.

Why?

Because things get lost when they're not connected. An idea nobody hears. A person nobody checks on. A question nobody asks. This doesn't fix that. It just makes it visible, so you can.

What does it mean?

"Gives to" means one thing helps another.
"Receives from" means it gets help from another.
"Not connected" means it's on its own. Nobody left behind is the goal.
"Everything is connected" means fair for all.

What does it know?

Nothing about you. It doesn't send data anywhere. Everything stays right here in your browser. If you clear your browser data, it's gone. It only knows what you put in it.
