Godding

You know how sometimes you have a bunch of things in your head and you can feel they're related but you can't see how?

That's what this is for. You write things down. Then you draw the lines between them. This helps that. That came from this.

When something is sitting there with no lines going in or out, it shows up differently. Not as a punishment. Just so you notice. So you can decide what to do about it.

· · ·

Below you'll see three things already here: You, Someone else, and Something in common. They're connected to each other. You give to Something in common. Someone else gives to it too. And it gives back to both of you.

That's the whole idea. Things help things. And when everything is helping something and being helped by something, it says "Everything is connected." That's the goal.

· · ·

You can add anything. A feeling. A person's name. A problem. A dream. A fear. Something you're grateful for. Then connect it to what it's related to.

It doesn't judge what you put in. It doesn't send it anywhere. It only lives in your browser, right here, right now. If you clear your browser data, it's gone. That's it.

The hand that gives is connected to the hand that receives.
